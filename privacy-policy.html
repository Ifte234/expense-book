Privacy Policy for Expense Book

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Expense Book, a personal and professional financial management application designed to help individuals and businesses track transactions, manage cash flow, organize trips, and monitor customer-related finances. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your data when using the Expense Book application ("the App").

Information We Collect

To provide a secure and efficient user experience, Expense Book may collect the following categories of data:

  • Personal Information: During account creation, we may collect your email address or phone number for user authentication, password recovery, and secure backup access.
  • Financial Data: We collect transaction-related data such as transaction titles, amounts just as numbers (for record), descriptions, associated dates, and any attached receipts or documents that you upload to keep financial records organized.
  • Device Permissions & Information: With your explicit consent, we may request access to:
    • Contacts: To allow quick addition of customers or trip members directly from your device.
    • Camera: To enable you to capture and attach receipt images.
    • Storage: To save, access, and retrieve media files linked to transactions.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app (e.g., feature usage, frequency, error reports) is collected anonymously to improve app performance, troubleshoot issues, and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect serves the following purposes:

  • To deliver app functionalities such as adding, editing, viewing, and sharing transactions, customers, and trips.
  • To generate financial reports and summaries based on your data for better financial decision-making.
  • To store and back up your data securely using Firebase, ensuring it remains accessible even if you change or reset devices.
  • To notify you about feature updates, reminders, and app-related communications through push notifications.
  • To provide in-app customer support and resolve technical issues promptly.
  • To deliver personalized advertisements through Google AdMob, subject to your device and ad settings.

Data Storage and Security

We prioritize the security and privacy of your data. Here’s how we ensure its protection:

  • Data is stored both locally on your device and backed up to the cloud using Firebase, a secure platform by Google.
  • All data transfers are encrypted using HTTPS, and cloud backups are secured with authenticated access.
  • Your backup and receipt image data are accessible only to your verified account and are not shared with anyone else unless explicitly initiated by you.
  • You can restore your data on any new device by verifying your account using a one-time password (OTP) in case if you log out or lost your device.You can not backupyour data if you have deleted your account.

Data Sharing

We are committed to maintaining your privacy. Your information is not sold or shared with third parties, except in the following scenarios:

  • With Firebase (Google Cloud) to facilitate secure storage and real-time data access.
  • With Google AdMob to serve in-app advertisements. No personally identifiable information (PII) is shared for ad personalization.

When you choose to share specific transactions with customers or participants, only view-only access is granted. Expense Book does not control how third-party recipients use the shared data. Please exercise discretion while sharing.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. You can delete or modify any transaction, customer, or trip at your convenience. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data:

  • Navigate to Settings > Delete Account.
  • Upon confirmation, all your stored data, including cloud backups and images, will be permanently and irreversibly deleted.

User Rights & Control

You maintain full control over your data and device permissions. As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and review the data you have stored in the app.
  • Modify or delete individual records or your entire account.
  • Export your data in readable formats (e.g., PDFs, spreadsheets, summaries) for customers managment.
  • Grant or revoke access to device features (camera, contacts, storage) via your phone’s settings at any time.
  • Manage cloud backup preferences and notification settings from within the app settings.
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements via your Google account settings or device ad settings.

To request access to your data or to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact.expensebook@gmail.com.

Advertising

We partner with Google AdMob to deliver advertisements within the app. Google may use anonymized cookies or device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your usage behavior and preferences. You can learn more and control ad personalization options through Google’s ad settings or visit this link.

Children’s Privacy

Expense Book is not designed or intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from minors. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take prompt action to delete the data.

Policy Updates

We may periodically revise this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal regulations, technological updates, or changes to our features. When updates are made:

  • You will be notified via in-app alerts or email.
  • The most recent version will always be accessible within the app under Settings > Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed of how your data is handled.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out to us through the in-app chat support or by emailing contact.expensebook@gmail.com.
